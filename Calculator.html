CALCULATOR

A Calculator is the instrument in which we calculate numbers like 2+2=4 etc.

Here are are few operators of calculator.

           (1)  +
(2)  -
(3)  *
(4)  /
(5) ()

Following are usage of operators

no.1: - is used to subtract numbers like 3-4=-1 etc.
no.2: * is used to multiply numbers like 3*3=6 etc.
no.3: / is used to divide numbers like 3/3=1 etc.
no.4: + is used to add numbers like 3+3=6 etc.
no.5: () are two operators that are used to difference b/w two numbers like (-3)*(-3)=6
